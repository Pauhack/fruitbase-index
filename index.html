Products

For now we are focused in commercializing the following products:

Lucuma

Its pulp has the color of the sunset and a creamy texture. It is usually eaten as a dessert in ice creams or smoothies. It is natural from Peru's coastal region and contains significant amounts of fiber and vitamin A.

Acai Berries

This small purple berries are being increasingly demanded by sports and health enthusiasts to make acai bowls, a dessert that combines acai with other fruits and nuts in a wonderfull way. It is considered a superfood due to its antioxidant properties. We sell it frozen.

Quinoa

This energy dense cereal is packed with all the essential amino acids. It was one of the most important Inka's staple grains and provided them the fuel needed to conquer new territories. Typicall grown in the Cordillera de los Andes highlands.
